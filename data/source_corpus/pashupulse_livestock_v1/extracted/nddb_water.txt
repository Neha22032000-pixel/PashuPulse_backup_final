NDDB Drinking Water for Dairy Animals

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Dairy animals need reliable access to clean drinking water. Water-intake changes should be interpreted together with feed, heat, behavior, dung, and other health signs.

Do not diagnose disease from drinking more or less water alone. Ask one key check and escalate only if red flags such as severe weakness, high fever, breathing trouble, or severe diarrhea appear.

Use for: routine water-intake questions and heat/weather observation.
