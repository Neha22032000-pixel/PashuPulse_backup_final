DAHD Livestock Health and Disease Control Programme

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Official livestock-health services are the right escalation path for severe, spreading, or uncertain animal-health problems. PashuPulse should frame itself as first-line support, not a replacement for trained animal-health services.

Use for: escalation, disease-control service framing, official animal-health support.

Do not use for: fake contacts, medicine names, dose guidance, injection advice, or claiming service availability in every village.
