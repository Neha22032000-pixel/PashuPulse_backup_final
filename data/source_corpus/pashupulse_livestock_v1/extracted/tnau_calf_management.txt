TNAU Cattle Care and Management

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Routine cattle care includes observing appetite, water, dung, standing or walking, cud chewing, milk change, and behavior.

Calving or calf-care contexts should stay clean, calm, and non-invasive unless trained help is present. Do not teach repositioning or hard pulling.

Use for: routine observation, cattle care, calf care, clean housing, calving safety boundaries.
