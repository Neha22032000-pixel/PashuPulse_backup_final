NDDB Calf Nutrition Grounding

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

For a healthy calf slow to start dry feed, first check feed freshness, water access, comfort, and whether the calf remains alert and active.

Escalate calf feeding issues when the calf becomes weak, stops drinking, has ongoing diarrhea, or cannot stand normally.

Do not provide ORS, antibiotic, supplement, or medicine doses.
