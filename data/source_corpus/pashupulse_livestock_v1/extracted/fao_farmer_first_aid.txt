FAO Farmer First Aid

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Farmer-level first aid should focus on preventing worsening, keeping the animal calm, observing signs, and seeking trained help when needed. It should remain supportive, not invasive.

First aid should not become medicine dosing, injection advice, forced treatment, surgery, or invasive handling.

Use for: calm first-line support, low-resource observation, escalation boundaries.
