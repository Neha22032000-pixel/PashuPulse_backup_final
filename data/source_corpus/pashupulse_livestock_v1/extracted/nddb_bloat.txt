NDDB Bloat Grounding

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Left-sided tight belly, restlessness, fast breathing, or severe abdominal distension after feed can be urgent in cattle or buffalo and should trigger trained-help escalation.

Oil, drenching, pipe insertion, puncturing, or cutting for suspected bloat should not be suggested to farmers.

Use for: bloat danger signs and myth correction.
