NDDB Aflatoxicosis

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Moldy, damp, clumped, or bad-smelling feed should be treated as suspicious and should not be casually fed to dairy animals. Feed contamination concerns should focus on dry storage, removing suspicious feed, and escalation if multiple animals are affected or severe signs appear.

Use for: spoiled feed, moldy feed, post-rain feed storage, reduced appetite with feed concern.

Do not use for: confirmed toxin diagnosis, binder dose, medicine dose, or milk safety guarantee.
