NDDB Clean Milk Production

Safe extracted notes for PashuPulse:

Clean milk production depends on clean animals, clean hands, clean utensils, and hygienic milking practices.

Abnormal milk such as visible clots, blood-like appearance, or bad smell should be kept separate while trained guidance is sought if red flags appear. Do not guarantee milk safety.

Use for: milking hygiene, abnormal milk caution, udder/milk safety boundaries.
